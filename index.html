About P. SOOD

Fewer things, made better.

P. SOOD brings together a small set of accessories, clothing and care essentials chosen for the same reason every time: they earn their place in daily use.

No seasonal drops for the sake of it, no filler products. Each category exists because it solves a real, ordinary problem — glare, a thinning wallet, a dry winter, a shirt that actually fits.

Material

Sourced, not guessed

Full-grain leathers, acetate frames and natural fibres, picked for how they hold up after the first month, not just the first photo.

Craft

Built to be used

Construction details — stitching, hinges, seams — are chosen for daily wear, not display.

Care

Plain ingredients

Hygiene and care essentials with short, legible ingredient lists and no unnecessary fragrance.
